Rotel Dip with a Lil' Twist

Mama's Favorite Appetizer

It ain't Mama's rotel dip unless you're using Queso and sausage for a little spice! Mama's Rotel Dip is the perfect start to dinner that will have you wanting to take some home! Don't have time to come grab some of Mama's? All you need is four ingredients! Let's get it!

Ingredients

  • 1lb queso blanco
  • 1lb sausage
  • 8oz cream cheese
  • 10oz can of rotel

Cooking Directions:

  1. Start by browning your sausage in a skillet until cooked through.
  2. As it’s cooking, cube the queso blanco cheese and the cream cheese.
  3. Once the meat is thoroughly cooked, stir in the cream cheese queso blanco mixture and the rotel.
  4. Stir regularly as it melts and then it’s ready to serve.

Extra Recipe Noteables:

  • Level: Beginner
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Preptime: 5 mins
  • Cooktime: 15 mins
  • Yields: About 3-4 servings
