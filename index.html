Mama Foreman's Kitchen

The first of this recipe was made homemade straight outta Mama Foreman's kitchen in 1963. Mama Foreman's dish brought many together for years during this hard time for African-Americans in the US. Mama Foreman had many recipes that would always bring smiles to everyone's face in her neighborhood. Every dish was from a recipe that was passed down for many generations. Years later the family opened up a small restaurant in the heart of Birmingham, AL: Mama Foreman's Kitchen. Mama Foreman always had a lil sumn sumn that she put in every dish to make it "Mama's", but here we have the basics along with a one or two ingredients that made Mama's dishes stand out from the rest of the pack.
